Coaching

One-to-one work for people who’ve been carrying something long enough that they need to say it out loud to someone who isn’t part of the situation.

Who this is for

You don’t need a particular title to work with me. The fit is in the problem: you’re navigating a role that requires you to hold space for others, as a leader, founder, community builder, teacher, or coach yourself, and the cost of holding that space has started to show.

  • Community leaders and founders navigating the moment where scale starts to strain the original warmth.
  • Coaches, facilitators, and teachers who are good at holding rooms but underpracticed at holding themselves.
  • Career-pivoters who can name who they were but are still working out who they’re becoming.

How I work

I don’t give you the answer. I ask you to find it, and I keep you accountable to the goals you’ve set for yourself. That means sitting with what you actually said, following where it leads, and being honest about what’s in the box before we start talking about what’s outside it.

Sessions are 60 minutes. I don’t sell packages — I start with one conversation and we decide from there.
